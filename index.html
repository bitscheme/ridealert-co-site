FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

Ride Alert is totally free and we will never show you any advertisements.

Do I need to register?

Nope! All you need to start using Ride Alert is your Strava account. We hate passwords, too üòé

What kind of personal data does the app collect?

We strive to make Ride Alert as noninvasive and hassle free as possible, storing your email address plus the public information provided to us by Strava via your profile. If you enable location services, your device location is stored each time you open or restore the app. See our Privacy Policy for more information.

I get too many push notifications. How can I reduce or disable them?

To turn off alerts, follow the instructions for your iOS or Android device. You can also mute individual users by accessing the app Settings screen.
