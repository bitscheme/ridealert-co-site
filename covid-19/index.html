Monday June 1, 2020

Public ride posting will resume today for all activities located in Oklahoma. This decision was made in consideration with the Phase 3 Open Up and Recover announcement by Governor Stitt:

Oklahoma To Begin Phase 3 of Open Up and Recover

In addition, private (by invitation) and shared link (excluded from the main feed) rides can be created anywhere you like. If cycling indoors is more your speed, you can now post virtual rides on platforms like Zwift and Rouvy.

Confused about the safety risks of riding in a group during the pandemic? USA Cycling has made some important recommendations that can help:

Riding in Groups

Group Ride Scenarios

Watch this page and our social media for further developments as more states communicate their plans to reopen.

Tuesday March 24, 2020

Yesterday I inactivated all upcoming rides and suspended public, link and private ride postings for reasons we are intimately aware of.  This step had been in the works since Sunday but was delayed due to an issue with one of the cloud providers.

I wish I could offer some guidance on a return timeline.  Cycling together is what we love to do, but the health of our communities is the highest priority.  I plan to keep the app online during this time of uncertainty and continue development.

Stay tuned for more updates via push notification, email and communications through our social media accounts.

For more information on cycling during the COVID-19 pandemic, refer to the bicycling.com article How to Ride Safely Amid Coronavirus Concerns
